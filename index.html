Kolrabyi

The aquatic race of Agoria, Kolrabyi live in Saliria, the submerged city. Their ancestors come from a magical influence on the creatures of the ocean.

Kolrabyi have become known as the ‘angels of the sea’ for their protective nature of their homeland, and all that live in it. The rare visitors they get are treated with kindness and respect, unless they are given a reason not to.

Kolrabyi have the evidence of their relation to the creatures around them predominant in their appearance. Many aquatic creature characteristics can be found on Kolrabyi, and no to Kolrabyi look the same.

Kolrabyi Attributes.

Age-

Kolrabyi live slightly longer than humans. You reach maturity at 20 years old, and live to 150.

Size-

Most Kolrabyi range from 6ft to 7ft. Your size is medium.

Speed-

Your walking speed is 30ft. You have a swimming speed of 40ft

Ability score increases-

You gain a +2 to your Dexterity score and a +1 to your constitution score

Alignment-

Most Kolrabyi lean toward neutral or lawful alignments, as they are very protective of their homes and people, and have high moral standards.

Ocean born child-

You gain the ability to breathe air and underwater. In addition, you get resistance to cold damage.

Darkness of the depths-

You gain a Blindsense of 30ft (this lets you detect invisible creatures, or those in the dark, as well as nearby walls, but does not negate disadvantage or advantage given by invisibility). You lose this blind sense when deafened, and it cannot be used to detect things in bright light. You are blind according to the normal human perception of eyesight while using this ability.

Magic of origin-

You learn the Acid splash cantrip. At third level, you learn create or destroy water and at fifth level you learn protection from poision. You can cast these spells without using a spell slot once per long rest. You choose whether you use intelligence, wisdom or charisma as your spellcasting ability.

Languages-

You can speak Common and primordial.
